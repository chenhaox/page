*Please note: This is the English version of my academic website. For the Chinese version, please visit [my official FAFU page](https://teacher.fafu.edu.cn/haochen/).*

Dr. Hao Chen is an Associate Professor at the College of Computer and Information Sciences, Fujian Agriculture and Forestry University (FAFU). He earned his Ph.D. (2024) and M.Sc. (2021) in System Innovation from Osaka University, Japan, under the supervision of [Prof. Kensuke Harada](http://www.hlab.sys.es.osaka-u.ac.jp/people/harada/) and [Prof. Weiwei Wan](https://wanweiwei07.github.io/). He received his B.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Macau (2018). 

His primary research focuses on advanced robotic manipulation and assembly planning, driving innovations for next-generation manufacturing systems. By intersecting intelligence with motion planning, his work seeks to enhance the autonomy and adaptability of industrial and service robots in complex environments.
